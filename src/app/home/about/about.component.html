New Prince Shri Bhavani College of Engineering and Technology

New Prince Shri Bhavani College of Engineering and Technology, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is a reputed institution dedicated to delivering quality technical education. It offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, emphasizing both innovation and practical learning. The college features modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and experienced faculty to ensure academic excellence. With a strong focus on holistic development, it actively promotes research, cultural participation, and industry collaboration to prepare students for successful careers.
