IQ Fusion

Get ready for IQ Fusion – the ultimate quiz battle that will push your brainpower, speed, and problem-solving to the limit! With two electrifying rounds ahead, each one will challenge different sides of your knowledge and thinking skills.

Event Structure

Round 1 : knowledge Sprint

The opening stage of IQ Fusion, where your grasp of concepts, speed of thought, and accuracy will be challenged to the fullest!

Round Format

  • Mode: Written Quiz
  • Total Questions: 30 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Time Allotted: 25 Minutes
  • Focus Areas: Technology & Science

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each question provides four options, with only one correct choice
  • No negative scoring – give your best attempt on every question!
  • All answers must be submitted within the specified duration.
  • The highest scorers will move forward to the next round, Visual Vault.

Round 2 : Visual Vault

The grand finale of IQ Fusion – Visual Vault! This round will put your observation and recognition skills to the ultimate test. Prepare for an exciting challenge where you’ll identify logos flashed on the screen.

Round Format

  • Mode : Visual Quiz
  • Task : Recognize and name the logos displayed
  • Time Allotted : 25 Minutes
  • Winner : The participant with the maximum correct identifications claims the title!

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each logo will appear for only a short duration.
  • Participants must respond verbally with the correct brand name.
  • Use of external help or internet is strictly prohibited.
  • The participant with the most correct responses will be crowned the IQ Fusion Champion!

Only the top three scorers will rise as champions, but every participant walks away with a certificate of participation. Get ready to prove your brilliance and shine bright at IQ Fusion!
