Eye On AI

Get ready for Eye On AI – "The Visionary Frame" is a dynamic AI-powered creative challenge that celebrates imagination, innovation, and digital artistry. In this event, participants will harness the power of AI tools to craft stunning images and captivating videos based on given themes. Through a blend of technology and creativity, contestants will bring their unique visions to life — transforming ideas into visuals that speak louder than words. Each round introduces a new twist, inspiring participants to push artistic boundaries and showcase the limitless potential of AI-generated art.

Event Structure

Round 1 : Visual Vibes

The first stage of Eye on AI invites participants to unleash their creativity through AI-powered image generation. Based on a given topic, contestants will use innovative AI tools to craft striking visuals that capture the essence of the theme. This round tests their imagination, prompt-building skills, and ability to translate ideas into artistic digital creations. Every detail matters — from concept clarity to visual appeal — as participants set the stage for an exciting journey through the world of AI creativity.

Round Format

  • Mode : Interactive creative activity
  • Task : Generate an image based on the given topic using AI tools
  • Team Size : Can be individual or a team of 2 members
  • Time Allotted: 20-25 Minutes

Round Format

  • Participants must use only AI tools approved or suggested by the event organizers
  • The given topic will be revealed at the start of the round, and participants must create their image within the allotted time.
  • The given topic will be revealed at the start of the round, and participants must create their image within the allotted time.
  • The prompt used for generation should be clearly mentioned or shown along with the final output.
  • Evaluation will be based on creativity, relevance to the theme, prompt clarity, and visual quality.

Round 2 : CineAI Vision

The second stage of Eye on AI takes creativity to the next level, as participants explore AI-powered video generation. Based on a given topic, contestants will transform ideas into motion, blending visuals, sound, and storytelling. This round highlights imagination, technical skill, and prompt precision, pushing participants to craft engaging and meaningful video content. With every second counting, this challenge keeps the energy high and the innovation flowing.

Round Format

  • Mode : Video creation based
  • Task : Generate a short video based on the given topic using AI tools
  • Submission System : The first team to submit their completed video within the time limit will get priority for evaluation.
  • Focus Area : Creativity, prompt clarity, visual storytelling, and technical execution

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team will receive the topic for video generation at the start of the round.
  • Teams must submit their completed video within the given time limit to be considered for evaluation (first submission gets priority).
  • Use of pre-made content or external assistance is strictly prohibited; all videos must be generated during the round.
  • Videos will be scored based on creativity, relevance, visual quality, and prompt clarity.

After the final round, the visionaries of Eye on AI will be crowned champions, while every participant will receive a certificate of participation. Step into the world of creativity, harness the power of AI, and let your imagination shine at Eye on AI!
