GenZ Thesis

Step into GenZ thesis, a dynamic paper presentation event designed to inspire innovation, research excellence, and technical creativity. It provides a platform for aspiring engineers to express their ideas and insights on emerging technologies through impactful PowerPoint presentations. GenZ thesis aims to nurture analytical thinking, presentation skills, and a passion for discovery among future innovators.

Event Structure

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of 3 members only.
  • Participants must select one domain from the list provided by the organizing committee.
  • The total presentation time is 5 minutes â€” 3 minutes for explanation and 2 minutes for jury questions.
  • Participants must submit their PPT on or before November 3
  • Only shortlisted participants will be permitted to present during the event.
  • The presentation must be original and free from plagiarism.
  • Teams should ensure the content is clear, relevant, and technically sound.
  • Any form of malpractice or external assistance during presentation will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the jury panel will be final.

Judging Criteria

  • Relevance to selected domain
  • Innovation and originality
  • Clarity and organization of presentation
  • Technical content and depth
  • Communication skills and Q&A performance

Winners will be awarded certificates and cash prizes. All other participants will receive participation certificates as a token of appreciation for their effort and enthusiasm.
