The Grid Vortex

Gear up for Grid Vortex – a high-energy tech challenge designed to test your teamwork, coding, problem-solving, and web development skills. Participants will face two dynamic rounds, each focusing on different tech abilities.

Event Structure

Round 1 : Logic Loop

The first stage of GridVortex focuses on collaboration, quick thinking, and technical accuracy. Teams of two will alternate between finding tech-related images and solving coding or logic problems, testing adaptability and teamwork under time pressure.

Round Format

  • Mode : Interactive Task
  • Team Size : 2 Members per team
  • Time Per Turn : 1 Minutes
  • Focus Areas: Technology recognition, Debugging, Logical Reasoning

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team member will swap roles every minute – one searches for tech-related images, while the other solves a code/logic problem
  • Tasks must be completed within the allotted time for each turn.
  • Accuracy and speed are key – incomplete or incorrect solutions may affect scores.
  • Teams performing the best move forward to Round 2.

Round 2 : WebForge

The grand finale of GridVortex – a 45-minute challenge to create a fully functional webpage. Teams must develop a front page, login page, and main page, and present their work. Creativity, functionality, and presentation skills will determine the winners.

Round Format

  • Mode : Practical Web Development
  • Duration : 45 Minutes
  • Task : Design and implement a webpage with:
    • Front Page
    • Login Page
    • Main Page
  • Presentation : Each team must present their webpage after completion.

Rules & Guidelines

  • All webpage elements should be functional and visually appealing
  • Teams must submit their work and perform a live presentation.
  • Use of pre-built templates is allowed, but originality will be scored.
  • Judges’ decision is final in case of tie-breakers.

The team demonstrating the best combination of speed, accuracy, problem-solving, web development, and presentation skills will be crowned GridVortex Champions. Top three teams receive prizes, and all participants earn certificates.
