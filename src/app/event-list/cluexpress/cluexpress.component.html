Clue Xpress

Participants will engage in a battle of wit, knowledge, and quick thinking through fun and challenging rounds that test their observation, vocabulary, and team coordination.

Round 1: Logo Lit

    Round Format

  • Participants will be shown images and logos, and they must identify the correct answer.

Round 2: Lexi Battle

This round combines vocabulary skills with strategy and fun challenges

    Round Format

  • Teams will pick a topic from the given list.
  • They will then bet against opponents on how many related words they can list within 1 minute.
  • If they meet their bet, they score a point; if not, the points go to the opponents.

Round 3: Hint & Hunt

    Round Format

  • Each team nominates one member as the describer and the other as the guesser.
  • The describer provides hints without directly stating the word, while the guesser must identify it.
  • The team that guesses the most words correctly wins the final round and secures the title.

    Event Details

  • Team Requirement : 2 Members per team
  • Materials : Bring your own pen
  • Rounds : LogoLit, Lexi Battle, and Hint & Hunt
  • Preparation : Participants are encouraged to sharpen their vocabulary, logical thinking, and observation skills.
