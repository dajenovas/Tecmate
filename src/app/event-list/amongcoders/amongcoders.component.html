Among Coders

Ready to prove you're the ultimate coder? Among Coders challenges you to a two-part battle of wits. First, test your knowledge in a high-stakes quiz round. Then, showcase your problem-solving skills as you debug and build solutions under pressure. Only the most skilled will rise to the top.

Event Structure

Round 1 : Code Cracker

The opening stage of "Among Coders," where your grasp of core programming concepts, speed of thought, and logical accuracy will be challenged to the fullest!

Round Format

  • Mode : Written Quiz / Online Quiz
  • Total Questions : 25-30 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Time Allotted : 20-25 Minutes
  • Focus Areas : Computer Science Fundamentals, Logical Reasoning, Programming Concepts (e.g., data structures, algorithms, syntax, common libraries), and Technology Trends.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each question will have four options, with only one correct answer.
  • There will be no negative marking, so answer every question.
  • Participants must submit their answers within the given time limit.
  • The top performers will advance to Round 2, the problem-solving challenge.

Round 2 : The Code Gauntlet

This is the ultimate test of a true programmer! This round will challenge your analytical thinking, problem-solving prowess, and debugging instincts. Prepare to face a series of coding and debugging tasks designed to separate the good from the great.

Round Format

  • Mode : Practical Coding and Debugging
  • Task : Participants will be given a set of 3-5 problems to solve. These will include a mix of new problem-solving challenges and pre-written code with logical or syntax errors that need to be debugged.
  • Time Allotted : 60-90 Minutes (depending on the difficulty of the problems)
  • Winner : The top three participants will be chosen based on the number of problems solved and the efficiency of their solutions (judged by speed and accuracy).

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants can use any standard programming language (Python, Java, C++, etc.)
  • All solutions must be submitted through a designated platform (e.g., a shared folder, an online judge, or a local submission system).
  • Participants are not allowed to use external resources like the internet, generative AI, or outside help.
  • Winners will be determined by a combination of the number of correct solutions and the time of submission.
  • The decisions of the judges are final.

Only the top three coders will be crowned as champions, but every participant will walk away with a certificate of participation. Get ready to put your coding skills to the ultimate test and prove you're among the best!
