Booyah BlitZ

Battle on a cursed island where Echo Cores grant power—but expose you. Survive ghost storms, fight corrupted foes, and use the new character Vira to disable enemy skills. Only the strong escape the Echo Zone.

Event Structure

Round 1 : Battle Royale (BR) Match

A battle royale set on a massive vertical map with floating islands and gravity zones. Players must climb while fighting off enemies and rising chaos from below. New gear like jet boots and antigravity grenades change how you move and fight. Only those who master the skies survive.

Round Format

  • Mode : BR match
  • Squad Match
  • Total 12 Squads : Limited Slots
  • Top 2 Squads will be selected to Round 2

Round Format

Round 2 : Clash Squad Match

Two squads face off in night-mode maps with limited vision and tactical gear like flash drones and silent footsteps. Players earn intel points each round to unlock special gadgets and perks. Strategy and stealth decide the victor in this intense 4v4 mode.

Round Format

  • Mode : Clash Squad Match
  • Play 4V4 Clash Squad match with opponent team.
